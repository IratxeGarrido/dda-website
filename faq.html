Blind Duck Dating

How does Blind Duck Dating work?

You apply for the pool, we curate a group, and selected people are invited to a live event.

How many people are usually at one event?

Usually around 8 hand-picked participants, so it stays personal and manageable.

How do matches happen?

After the event, you privately choose who you’d like to connect with. If it’s mutual, you get matched.

Is registration free?

Yes, joining the pool is currently free.

Poly Speed Dating

Who is Poly Speed Dating for?

It is designed for polyamorous people and people open to non-monogamous connection styles.

Is it moderated?

Yes. The event is guided live, so the structure feels clear and welcoming.

How often does PSD happen?

Usually every few months, depending on planning and capacity.

Can I connect romantically and socially?

Depending on the setup, yes — the format can include both romantic and social matching.

PolyFest

What is PolyFest?

PolyFest is a broader community event with workshops, talks, and connection-focused programming.

Is PolyFest only about dating?

No. It is also about community, learning, discussion, and meeting like-minded people.

Can I come alone?

Absolutely. Many people come on their own and meet others there.

Will there be more PolyFest events?

That’s the plan. Follow updates through the website and mailing list.
