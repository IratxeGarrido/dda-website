FAQ’s

The short version of how Blind Duck Dating works.

Is this in person?

Yes. That’s the whole point. You meet people live at a real event in Berlin.

Is this just another dating app?

No. The event happens in person. The app is only used afterward for mutual matching.

How many people are there?

Usually 8 hand-picked candidates per event, so it stays personal and not overwhelming.

How does matching work?

After the event, you indicate who you’d like to connect with. If it’s mutual, you get matched the next day.

Is it only for romantic matches?

Not always. Depending on the event, you may be able to indicate romantic or social interest.

Where can I see events?

On the Events page.
