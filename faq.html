🎁 Duck Dating Apps (DDA)

Invite someone to join and get rewarded once they participate.

How it works: You refer a person → They apply and get accepted → After they attend an event, you receive a notification

Your reward: 1 free spot for a future event

Must be used within 3 months

💕 Poly Speed Dating (PSD)

Know someone perfect for PSD? Invite them to apply.

Scenario 1: Your referral gets accepted, but you don't → You get a guaranteed seat for the next PSD event (regular price applies)

Scenario 2: Both accepted for the same event → You get a €5 discount on your ticket

Rewards must be used within 12 months

General Guidelines

  • ✓ Referrals are free — No cost to participate
  • ✓ Rewards are personal — Cannot be transferred
  • ✓ Limited time use (expire after 3-12 months)
  • ✓ We decline misuse or fake referrals (and sanction users who miss-use)
