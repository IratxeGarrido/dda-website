DDA Referral Program

Invite someone to join Duck Dating Apps and get rewarded once they participate in an event.

How It Works

1

You refer a person

Tell them about Duck Dating Apps and invite them to apply for an event

2

They apply and get accepted

They apply and are selected to participate in the event (don't forget to mention your name!)

3

After they attend an event

Once they participate and complete the event, you're notified of your reward

Your Reward

1 Free Event Spot

Use your free spot for any standard DDA event in the future

Important Notes

  • •

    The referred person must be a new participant

  • •

    Reward is granted only after they attend (not just apply or get accepted)

  • •

    Free spot is valid for a standard event ticket

  • •

    Reward must be used within 3 months

  • •

    Referral rewards are not transferable

  • •

    Limit: 1 successful referral every 3 months

  • •

    We reserve the right to decline misuse or fake referrals

Why we do this

DDA grows through real connections — people who already trust each other create better events. When you refer someone, you're essentially vouching for them, which means they'll likely be a great fit for the event and the group dynamic. The best way to build a community is by having people bring their trusted friends into the mix.

Ready to refer a friend?

Share Duck Dating Apps with your friends and let them know to mention your name when they apply.
