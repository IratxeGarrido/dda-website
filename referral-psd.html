Poly Speed Dating

PSD Referral Program

Know someone who'd be a great fit for Poly Speed Dating? Invite them to apply and both the community and you can benefit.

How It Works

1

Your referred person gets accepted, but you don't

✅ You receive: A guaranteed seat for the next PSD event

Regular ticket price still applies

2

Both you and your referred person get accepted for the same event

✅ You receive: A €5 discount on your ticket for that event

Important Notes

  • •

    The referred person must be a new applicant who mentions your name when applying

  • •

    Referral rewards apply only once per referred person

  • •

    Guaranteed seats are valid for the next regular PSD edition only

  • •

    Rewards must be used within 12 months

  • •

    Discounts cannot be combined with other promotions unless stated otherwise

  • •

    Referral rewards are not transferable

  • •

    Limit: 1 successful referral every 3 months

  • •

    We reserve the right to decline misuse or fake referrals

Why we do this

PSD grows best through trusted recommendations. Great people often know other great people. When someone brings their friends into our community, they're vouching for a shared vibe and values. This creates a higher quality event experience for everyone, and it means our community grows authentically through real connections rather than just marketing.
